"The United Nations (UN) is
the closest thing we have to
a world government"

  • founded on a legal document (The UN Charter),
  • a Court to resolve disputes between members,
  • a Parliament of states (the General Assembly),
  • and crucially, it can authorize collective enforcement of its will (via the Security Council)

Our international law are
based on UN Treaties & Custom
